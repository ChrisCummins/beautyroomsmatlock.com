About Us

Our Mission

At The Beauty Rooms, our sole aim is to deliver a professional an unrivalled quality of service to our clients using a comprehensive variety of up to date treatments and products to achieve total wellbeing, beauty and youthfulness.

If you want to feel and look your very best, why not make your appointment at the Beauty Rooms? It really doesn't get better than this.

Over 15 years of Experience and Quality Service

Set in the heart of Derbyshire, one of the country's most picturesque counties and on the edge of the beautiful Peak National Park, the Beauty Rooms has been caring for clients for over 15 years from many different parts of the country.

Although we deliver a quality spa salon feel, we are unlike the spa farms where the aim is to cater for many and the menu of services can be so large that not one reaches the highest standards of expectation.

On an appointment only basis, the very latest beauty treatments and products from the best of the skincare manufacturers such as Paris based CARITA and ELEMIS are offered. Fabulous make up comes from the German company, ART DECO.

Whether the aim is pure beauty, the treatment of skin issues, make-up, eye lashes, nails, massage, manicures or pedicures, we can guarantee you will enjoy an experience that will be hard to match anywhere else.... book your appointment today and we'll make you feel special.
