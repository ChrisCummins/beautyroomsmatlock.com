Luxury Treatments

The Beauty Rooms in Matlock, Derbyshire has been offering high class beauty therapy and retail for 15 years. Here, our focus is on programmes of individual specialist skin care, beauty treatments and massage for our clients utilising the latest anti-ageing techniques and the best products to promote youthful skin and total wellbeing.

Operated by experienced facial specialist Julie Walford, our salon provides all of the services expected by the most discerning, but at a quality level rarely achieved.

Julie's rare and natural empathy with her clients' needs means that the whole experience can be rarely matched elsewhere.

How to find us

Take the A6 from Matlock towards Bakewell. Approx 2 miles from Matlock look for the Red House Carriage Museum on the left, then take the next right hand turn proceeding up Greenaway Lane.

Look out for Greenaway Lane School on the left and The Beauty Rooms is approximately quarter of a mile past this on the right hand side.
